Yuhanxiao Ma

Research Artist

She works with generative art, multimedia installations, 3d scanning/fabrication, and assistive edge devices. Her interdisciplinary works examine reality and truth, specifically the extraordinary of the mundane, seemingly opposing ideas and the construction of authenticity in this lie-prone, post-truth era. She also focuses on the paradoxical relationship between humans and machines (technology) and would like to use her artworks and research to investigate its socio-political implications.
