In The Eyes of Machines

The World Encapsulated in Pixels

Python, Processing, Images extracted from web browser based on user input

In the Eye of Machines is a project that investigates how machines perceive humans and their surroundings through pixel and RGB data, questioning how these digital ‘eyes’ reconstruct our reality. By showcasing images in a stochastic manner within a 3D space, the project reflects the inherent unpredictability and abstract nature of how machines ‘understand’ or ‘piece together’ human imagery. Challenging traditional views of human centrality in intelligence and perception, this project highlights the dynamic nature of machine interpretations, showcasing the evolving interaction between technology and human understanding.
